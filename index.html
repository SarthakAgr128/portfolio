Who I Am

I'm a cybersecurity professional who lives at the intersection of offensive security and software engineering. I spend my days finding vulnerabilities, building security tooling, and competing in CTFs — and my nights reading RFCs and threat intelligence reports.

My philosophy is simple: understand the attacker's mindset to build stronger defenses. Whether it's a web app pentest, a red team engagement, or an open-source security tool, I approach every problem with curiosity, rigor, and a hacker's creativity.

When I'm not in a terminal, you'll find me contributing to security communities, mentoring newcomers, or writing about what I've learned.
