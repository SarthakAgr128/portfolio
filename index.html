A bit about me

I grew up taking things apart to see how they worked — clocks, radios, my parents' patience. That curiosity never left; it just moved to computers, puzzles, and figuring out why things break.

By day I work in cybersecurity — finding vulnerabilities, building tools, and making systems safer. But that's only one chapter. Outside of work, I'm someone who gets unreasonably excited about a random trip, new tech innovations, and makes reels like they're going to change the world.

I believe the best conversations happen over food, the best ideas come from unexpected places, and the best people are the ones who stay curious. If you're reading this, you might be one of them.
