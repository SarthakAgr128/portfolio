About — the human behind the keyboard

Sarthak sums it up

I grew up pulling things apart to see how they tick — clocks, radios, and eventually software stacks that resisted every assumption. That habit turned into a career in cybersecurity: find the irreproducible crack, understand it, and help teams fix it before someone less friendly does.

I care about clear writeups, tooling that actually ships, and teaching others to read a trace without losing their soul. When not onscreen I’m brewing pour-overs, playing strategy games, or regretting that one misclick in a CTF final.
